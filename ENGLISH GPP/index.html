      Diabetes Awareness in the UAE

      The United Arab Emirates (UAE) is witnessing a growing public health crisis as diabetes rates soar to alarming levels. With an estimated 16.3% of the UAE adult population living with diabetes, the country ranks among the top ten globally for diabetes prevalence The high incidence of this condition has placed a significant burden on the healthcare system, making diabetes a public health priority for the UAE government. Contributing factors such as sedentary lifestyles, unhealthy diets, and genetic predisposition are exacerbating the problem, leading to both Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes becoming more prevalent.source
